Notci

Hi, today I want to show you my idea of a new protocol, for which I look for partners. A handful should already be enough. My name is Armin, but on the net you can find me under the nick bebna, but let us come back to the topic:

The Problem

Today, Twitter is on the most important ways of communication, but is it central and under closed development. Which makes it a ticking bomb for me. Especially after the owner decided to give it's own client advantages over other alternative client implementations.

The Solution

A open and decentral protocol will provide a good fundation for the future of short message communication and micro blogging.
Those type of protocols are today not something unknown or hard, there are already known and proven implemenations:

  • Bittorrent
  • Bitcoin
  • Tor
  • ...

The Rough Requirements

But unfortunately these protocols don't fit or target the needs a short message or micro blogging service has to provide. The creation of posts or messages has to be easy and natural to the user, there is a need for public and private distribution, those messages have short life time and will be read or written by mostly mobile and often multiple devices from the same user.

The Way To Go

But thanks to the already known implemenations, one only has to pick the needed parts and get them to work together in a harmonic way.
This makes it easy to imagine the resulting work and protocol; there a part of Bittorrent for distributed metadata, here somethings from Bitcoin and so on.

Things To Do

In the end all what we have to do is identifieng the features we ant to implement, pick the parts needed and merge them together in a to be published protocol.
In the end, I could do this on my own, but would this be a real protocol then? I think not. Thats why I'm inviting you to this project and look forward to your comments!
